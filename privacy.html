Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 7, 2024

At LongForm AI, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us when you:

  • Fill out a contact form (Name, Email, Company Info).
  • Register for an account or subscribe to a service.
  • Provide brand guidelines or research materials for content creation.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain our content services.
  • Process payments and prevent fraud.
  • Communicate with you regarding your account, drafts, and updates.
  • Improve our website and service offerings based on usage data.

3. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services to help us operate our business:

  • Payment Processing: We use Stripe to process all subscription payments. We do not store your credit card information on our servers.
  • Analytics: We use industry-standard analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics) to understand how visitors interact with our site. These tools may use cookies to collect anonymous usage data.

4. GDPR Notice (For EU Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have certain rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right of Access: You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: You can request that we correct inaccurate data.
  • Right to Erasure: You can request that we delete your personal data.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Where we rely on your consent to process data, you can withdraw it at any time.

To exercise these rights, please contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@longform-ai.com.

5. Security

We implement administrative, technical, and physical security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Your Choices

You can opt-out of marketing emails at any time by clicking the "unsubscribe" link. You can also disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features of the Service may not function properly.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@longform-ai.com.
