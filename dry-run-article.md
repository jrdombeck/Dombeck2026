## Why Shoppers Abandon Carts (The Data-Backed Truth)

According to the latest Baymard Institute studies, the top reasons for cart abandonment are:

1. **Extra costs too high** (shipping, tax, fees) — 48%
2. **The site wanted me to create an account** — 24%
3. **I didn't trust the site with my credit card information** — 18%
4. **Checkout process was too long or complicated** — 17%
5. **I couldn't see or calculate total order cost up-front** — 16%
